Environment


The city may be covered by a dome, but that doesn't mean it can't have an environment. Houses have flowers and plants in pots and gardens, and trees are planted between houses and facilities. When using wood, we make sure to plant two seeds for every tree we cut. Citizens are allowed to keep pets such as cats, dogs, rabbits, or any other domesticated animal. Our dome constantly maintains the required light and humidity levels for plants to grow, but if you want to experience the great outdoors you're free to leave the city for up to six months at a time. There's nothing to lose!
