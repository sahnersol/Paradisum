"The Perfect Earth"

Paradisum, Latin for "Paradise," is a perfect, self-sustaining community driven on solar energy. Citizens can get married and have as many children as they want, as they can all contribute to the community. Our flag is olive and white, symbolising peace and purity. The city is protected and sustained through a dome of solar panels beneath a layer of reinforced, magnifying glass, concentrated the sun's light and focusing it into energy.
