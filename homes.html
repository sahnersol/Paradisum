Homes


Our homes run on solar energy, and do not polute the environment. The light from the sun travels to the lighting of the house, so lightbulbs do not need to be replaced. For televisions, the sun's light is shined through color-changing pixels to bring any show, movie, or video game right to your home. Citizens can get a house on whatever street they want so they can live near family and friends. Houses are free, so there's no reason not to move!
