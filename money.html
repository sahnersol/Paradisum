Currency


Our currency are Petras, and they come in units of 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, and 1,000. They are made of wood from our tree farms and are systematically carved using a solar-power machine, so they don't use any limited resources. Our jobs offer plenty of Petras, so you can spend what you want while working how you want.
