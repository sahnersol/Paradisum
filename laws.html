Government

Our city is designed to be as safe and pleaserable as possible, and in order to establish that some laws need to be put into place. If the below laws are violated by a person five or more times, they will have to leave the community. The main ten laws can not be changed, but others can be by vote of citizens. The government is a democracy. Laws are enforced by police officers who do not harm people, but incapacitate them. These laws are easy to follow, and protect citizens and their rights.

  1. If they are 21 or older, all citizens must have a job to contribute to the community.
  2. Citizens are allowed to say whatever they want as long as it isn't hate speech.
  3. Citizens are allowed to believe whatever they want.
  4. Citizens are not allowed to harm eachother or their possesions in any way.
  5. All children must receive proper education.
