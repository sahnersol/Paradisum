Transporation


To get around with speed but without polution or risk, citizens use electric cars driven on solar energy. They harvest this energy at charging stations, where they easily click into place and are charged by the sun's energy. These cars are covered with a near-indestructable Linex coating to prevent damage. Oxide compounds have also been added to the windows to prevent scratching and cracking, making them almost as hard as steel. Our cars get you wherever you want with no polution or possible injury.
